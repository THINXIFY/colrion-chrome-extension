Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

Thank you for using Colrion, a Chrome extension developed by THINXIFY. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Colrion uses, how it is processed, and the choices available to you.

1. Overview

Colrion is a browser extension that helps users pick colors from webpages, extract website color palettes, generate related color combinations, and manage saved color collections. Colrion is designed with privacy in mind and only accesses webpage content when you explicitly use its features.

2. Information We Collect

Colrion does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or identification documents.

When you use Colrion, the extension may locally store:

  • Saved colors
  • Favorite colors
  • Custom color palettes
  • User preferences
  • Extension settings
  • Backup and restore data created by you

This information is stored locally using Chrome's extension storage to provide a consistent user experience across browser sessions.

3. Website Content Access

When you choose to extract colors from a webpage or use the color picking features, Colrion temporarily accesses the currently active webpage only to perform the requested operation.

This access is used solely to:

  • Pick colors from webpages.
  • Analyze webpage colors.
  • Generate website color palettes.
  • Display requested color information.

Colrion only performs these actions after they are initiated by you.

4. Permissions

Colrion uses a limited set of Chrome extension permissions to provide its core features.

  • activeTab
    Temporarily accesses the currently active webpage when you request color picking or webpage color extraction.
  • scripting
    Executes extension scripts on the active webpage when required to support user-initiated functionality such as webpage color analysis and visual feedback.
  • storage
    Saves your settings, saved colors, favorites, custom palettes, and other extension preferences locally.

5. Data Sharing

Colrion does not sell your personal information.

Colrion does not use your data for advertising purposes.

Colrion does not share your saved colors or preferences with third parties.

6. Data Security

Colrion is designed to minimize the amount of information it stores. User data saved by the extension remains under your control and is intended only to provide the extension's functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

Colrion may provide links to external websites such as the official website, GitHub repository, documentation, or support resources. These websites operate under their own privacy policies, and THINXIFY is not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Colrion is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements, feature updates, legal requirements, or changes to the extension. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or Colrion, please contact us.
